Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard – Report

1. Introduction
   This report presents an analytical overview of Netflix’s content library using an interactive dashboard. The objective of the analysis is to understand content distribution, genre dominance, country-wise production, duration trends, and data completeness. The dashboard enables stakeholders to make informed decisions regarding content strategy and regional focus.

2. Dataset Overview
   The dataset includes information related to:
       Show ID
       Title
       Genre
       Country
       Release Year
       Rating
       Duration
       Director
   The data has been cleaned and structured to support meaningful visual analysis.

3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
   The dashboard highlights the following KPIs:
   Count of Show ID:
      Represents the total number of movies and TV shows available in the dataset.
   Count of Rating:
      Indicates the total number of rating categories used for Netflix content.
   %GT Count of Director:
      Shows the percentage of titles with director information, reflecting data quality and completeness.
   Sum of Duration Value:
      Displays the total combined duration of all Netflix content, representing the overall content volume.

4. Genre-wise Analysis
    4.1 Sum of Duration by Genre
      Drama has the highest total duration, indicating longer content formats.
      Comedy and Documentary follow, showing consistent audience demand.
      Thriller, Action, and Horror contribute moderately.
    4.2 Count of Titles by Genre
      Drama dominates in terms of number of titles.
      Documentary and Comedy have significant representation.
      Sci-Fi, Horror, and Action appear in smaller proportions.
   This indicates Netflix’s strong focus on storytelling and long-form content.

5. Country-wise Content Distribution
   A world map visual represents Netflix’s global content presence:
   The United States and India are the largest contributors.
   European countries such as France and Germany show moderate production.
   Content is well distributed across multiple regions, reflecting Netflix’s global strategy.
   A country slicer enables users to filter the analysis by selected countries for focused insights.

6. Year-wise Content Trend
   The year-wise duration analysis reveals:
   A steady increase in content production in recent years.
   Peak content volume between 2022 and 2024, showing aggressive expansion.
   This growth reflects Netflix’s investment in original and regional content.

7. Key Insights
   Netflix heavily prioritizes Drama and Comedy genres.
   Content production has increased significantly in recent years.
   The platform maintains a strong global footprint, led by the USA and India.
   Data completeness is relatively high, ensuring reliable analysis.

8. Conclusion
   The Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Netflix’s content strategy. It highlights Netflix’s emphasis on high-demand genres, consistent global expansion, and continuous growth in content volume. These insights can support strategic decisions related to content investment, regional expansion, and audience targeting.
